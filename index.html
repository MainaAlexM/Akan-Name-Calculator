Akan Names

Akan names are derived from the ghanaian traditonal naming system. Each name represents which day of the week one was born. Different genders have differing Akan names for their respective days of Birth.

Sample Akan Names

# Day Male Female
0 sunday Kwasi Akosua
1 Monday Kwadwo Adwoa
2 Tuesday Kwabena Abenaa
3 Wednesday Kwaku Akua
4 Thursday Yaw Yaa
5 Friday Kofi Afua
6 Saturday Kwame Ama
